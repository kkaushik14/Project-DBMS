About Library Management System

A modern, user-friendly platform for managing library resources, empowering both students and admins with seamless book management, notifications, and analytics.

For Students

  • Sign up and log in securely (password & OTP)
  • Issue books online with due date and payment tracking
  • Receive email notifications for issued books
  • Modern dashboard to view all issued books
  • Personalized experience with avatars and notifications

For Admins

  • Admin authentication (password & OTP)
  • Track all students and issued books
  • View and manage student records
  • Dashboard with analytics and quick actions
  • Role-based access and secure management

Tech Stack

Node.js Express MySQL HTML Tailwind CSS JavaScript

Key Features

  • Unified, modern UI with dark mode and Inter font
  • Separate dashboards and authentication for students and admins
  • Animated notifications and session persistence
  • Book issue and return management with email alerts
  • Admin analytics and student record management
  • Secure, role-based access throughout the system
